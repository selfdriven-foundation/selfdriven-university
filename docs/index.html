What is the selfdriven Foundation?

Founded in 2019, the selfdriven Foundation is a nonprofit organisation dedicated to empowering communities to actively shape their own development through education, facilitation, and innovative decentralised technology.

Its work has been supported by the entityOS Better Outcomes Fund, grants from programs like Cardano Catalyst, and contributions from a growing network of community members and partners who share its vision for locally driven, collaborative solutions
